## 🔍 Why GroupWhat?

Most websites that share **WhatsApp group links** have a common problem — they publish links once and never maintain them. Over time, many of those links become full, expired, or invalid, wasting users’ time.

GroupWhat is built to solve this.

- 🔄 We automatically check WhatsApp group links on a regular basis  
- ❌ Expired, revoked, or full links are automatically removed  
- ✅ Only working and joinable links are kept in the directory  
- ⏱️ Saves users from trying multiple broken or outdated links  

Instead of browsing outdated lists, users can rely on GroupWhat for a more reliable way to find **active WhatsApp group links**.
